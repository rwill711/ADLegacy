Adventure Legacy is a 2.5d Tactics Game, using GODOT 4.6.2:
Key Points:
-Clone of Final Fantasy Tactics Advanced, including job system, map-tile placing system
-Additionally we will implement attacks and skills that interact w/ the environment 
-Main piece of balancing will be a CPU tracking system that will allow the NPCs to adapt to your play style, called "FOIL" system. There should be a tracker in the background that keeps note of your most used attacks and breaks them into archetype 'playstyles' that the computer will adapt to play against. To start; we will have level 0-4 with 0 being no adaptation and 4 being the CPU is tailored to beat your most common tactics. For example; if you are a fire mage character and in all of your encounters you have used fire spells, the computer will at level 1 have a fire resistance potion that makes the attacks less effective, at level 4 the enemies would be equipped with armor that negates fire damage. 

Game is also rogue-like, with death being a main part used to push the story forward. The player will leave behind 'legacies' based on how much renown they accumulate (renown is detailed more in creative log). For example, if you are a great warrior and you 'retire' with a lot of renown, you might be able to establish a statue in a city and leave a piece of armor or a weapon at the location w/ a requirement for a certain amount of renown to remove it. The next character then has a 'Legacy' quest that can allow them to achieve just that. Characters with enough renown can also have 'legacy' characters that inherit specific skills, or have increased stats based on their predecessor. Almost like a New Game+ concept as it goes on. But the weapons and armor you unlock or forge will also be available to the CPU as time goes on, or a unique weapon you made might fall into the hands of the person who defeated you, which will then be able to be looted if you defeat them in the next life.
